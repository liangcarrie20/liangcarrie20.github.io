Seattle Great Wheel

This website is about the Seattle Great Wheel.

In 2014, my friends and I visited Seattle. One evening, we decided to take a stroll on the pier. We saw the Seattle Great Wheel from a distance and walked toward it. We went on the Seattle Great Wheel and enjoyed the amazing view of downtown Seattle.

From that day forward we decided to quit our jobs and start a business bringing tourists to the Seattle Great Wheel.

Seattle Great Wheel Trivia:

  • The inauguration ceremony and opening to the public was June 29, 2012
  • Seattle was the third city in North America to offer a wheel
  • There is one luxury gondola with red leather seats and a glass floor
